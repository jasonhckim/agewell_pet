Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

Welcome to AgeWell Pet. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Pet profiles: name, breed, birthdate, health data (locally stored)
  • App usage data: device type, app version, OS (anonymized)
  • Optional user input: notes, reminders, health logs
  • Contact data if you email us: name, email address

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide app functionality and personalized care guidance
  • To improve performance and user experience
  • To send support or update messages if requested

Data Storage

We store all data locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not collect or upload your pet data to any cloud service unless explicitly enabled in future updates.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools such as Expo, Firebase (for analytics/crashes), and email services. These may collect limited anonymized data for diagnostics and performance.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your app data anytime from your device.
  • If you have concerns, contact us at: privacy@agewellpet.com

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We'll notify users of major changes via app update notes or email (if collected).
