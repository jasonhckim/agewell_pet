AgeWell Pet

Smart Pet Health Tracker

AgeWell Pet is your intelligent pet care assistant, helping you manage your dog or cat's health throughout every stage of life. Track milestones, health logs, vet reminders, and more — all tailored to your pet’s breed and age.

Core Features

  • Multi-pet profiles with photos, stats, and breed-specific data
  • Veterinary-based milestone tracking with breed-specific care plans
  • Health logs: mood, symptoms, medication, appetite, and more
  • Smart reminders for vet visits, meds, grooming, and feeding
  • Visual health trends and export-ready logs
  • Modern, intuitive interface – works on iOS, Android, and Web

Built With

  • React Native with Expo SDK 53
  • Expo Router & AsyncStorage
  • TypeScript, modular components, Lucide icons
